1. Overview

This policy explains when refunds are available for Estimix paid access, licenses, subscriptions, and related Nebulix services.

2. Refund Window

Refunds may be requested within 7 days of the first paid purchase if you have not used the product heavily or received custom onboarding.

Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method where possible. Payment providers and banks may take additional time to post the refund.

3. Non-Refundable Items

  • Consumed AI, transcription, generation, export, download, or processing usage.
  • Custom onboarding, custom setup, consulting, training, or support already delivered.
  • Accounts suspended or terminated for abuse, unlawful use, chargeback misuse, or Terms violations.
  • Currency conversion differences, bank fees, or third-party payment fees outside Nebulix control.

4. How to Request a Refund

Email hello@nebulixcloud.com from the purchase email. Include the app name, account email, invoice or payment reference, purchase date, and reason for the request.

5. Disputes

Please contact Nebulix before opening a chargeback so we can investigate. Unresolved payment disputes may result in license suspension while the dispute is reviewed.
