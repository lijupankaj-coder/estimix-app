Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Estimix ("we", "us", "our") is a web application provided by Nebulix. This policy explains how we handle information when you use Estimix.

Data We Collect

  • Email address — only when you purchase a plan or check access. Stored in our Supabase database to manage your licence.
  • Usage count — stored locally in your browser (localStorage) to track free estimate usage. Never sent to our servers.
  • Estimate history & client data — stored entirely in your browser's localStorage. We never receive or store your project briefs, estimates, or client details.

AI Processing (Ollama)

Estimix sends your project brief to a local Ollama AI model running on your own machine. No brief content is ever sent to Nebulix servers or any third-party AI service. Your data stays on your device.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to store your preferences, estimate history, client rate cards, and access status. We do not use tracking cookies.

Google AdSense

We display Google AdSense advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve personalised ads based on your browsing. You can opt out via Google's ad settings.

Your Rights

You can clear all local data by clearing your browser's localStorage for this site. To request removal of your email from our access records, contact hello@nebulixcloud.com.

Contact

Questions? Email us at hello@nebulixcloud.com.
