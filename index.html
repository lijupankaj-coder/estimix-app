How to Use Estimix

Quick guide to get the most out of your estimate builder.

Getting Started

1
Set your workspace — on first visit, enter your company name. This keeps all your data (clients and estimates) private to your workspace.
2
Add a client — go to the Clients tab and click "Add New Client".
3
Upload your letterhead — go to the Letterhead tab and upload your company letterhead image. It will appear at the top of every PDF you download.

Creating an Estimate

1
Fill in Project Details — choose a client, add a project name, reference number, currency, and tax rate.
2
Add deliverables — type each deliverable name and enter its cost. Click "+ Add Deliverable" to add more rows. The preview updates live as you type.
3
Add notes — any payment terms, assumptions, or remarks go in the Notes field and appear on the PDF.
4
Build & Download — click Build Estimate to lock in the totals, then download as Excel or PDF. The PDF includes your letterhead.
💡 Tip: Set your accent color using the color swatch in the sidebar — this also changes the PDF title bar color to match your brand.

Workspace & Sharing

1
All data is stored in your browser under your workspace. Click the Estimix logo 5 times to open Settings and see your workspace code.
2
To use the same workspace on another device or browser — on the setup screen, click "Already have a workspace code?" and enter your code.
