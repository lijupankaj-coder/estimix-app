Estimix offers free access of 3 estimate generations. Paid access is: monthly and yearly Pro access for unlimited estimates; prices are displayed in the app.

2. Activation and Renewal

Paid Estimix access is activated manually after payment confirmation. Yearly access runs for the paid term, and renewal requires a new payment or explicit renewal confirmation.

Taxes, VAT, and currency conversion may apply depending on your location and payment method.

3. Cancellation

To cancel, downgrade, or stop renewal, email hello@nebulixcloud.com from the account or purchase email. Access normally remains active until the end of the paid term unless the account is terminated for misuse.

4. Upgrades and Downgrades

Plan changes may be handled manually. Upgrades can be activated immediately after payment confirmation. Downgrades usually apply at the next billing or renewal period unless Nebulix confirms otherwise.

5. Failed Payments and Expiry

If payment fails, expires, or is not renewed, paid features may be disabled, limited, or returned to the free tier. Data retained in browser storage remains under your control unless you clear it.
